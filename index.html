About Me

I am a high honor roll student in the class of 2026 at Taft (CT) where I am also a proud two-year member of the school’s championship Varsity Field Hockey Team. Taft’s field hockey program is exceptional. After an undefeated 2023 season, and a repeat of the Founders League and WNEPSAC Championships, we advanced to the semifinal of the Class A tournament and faced Nobles in a 2-1 win. We then traveled to Pomfret Academy for the Championship game where we won 3-2 and claimed the 2023 NEPSAC Champions title. I wear #7. In addition to starting for Taft, I also play for HTC under Coach Deb Brickey wearing #57 on the U19 RCC and NIT teams.
